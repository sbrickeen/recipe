Ingredients

- Ground Beef (Varies)

- Sliced Potatoes (Varies)

- One tub of Sour Cream (Optionl)

- One bag of Frozen Diced Potatoes

- One can of Cream of Mushroom Soup

- One bag of Shredded Cheddar Cheese

- One container of Cheesy Fried Onions (Optional)

Cooking Instructions

Step 1: Add potatoes and sour cream in a large bowl

Step 2: Mix in the mushroom soup in with the other ingredients

Step 3: Mix in the cheese

Step 4: Place ground beef in pan and cook thoroughly

Step 5: Bake until potatoes are golden brown and cheese begins to bubble

Step 6: Mix ground beef with the rest of the ingredients
